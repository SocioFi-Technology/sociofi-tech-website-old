# SocioFi Website Redesign Plan — Visionary Startup for Bangladesh

## 1. Positioning & Vision

**Positioning:** SocioFi is a **visionary startup** for Bangladesh with one mission: **ensure every enterprise and industry can harness the power of AI**. We build the **right solution** for each client using deep knowledge of the latest technology — no generic tooling; only fit-for-purpose AI.

**Core messages (to propagate everywhere):**
- Every enterprise and industry in Bangladesh can harness AI — we make it possible.
- We build the *right* solution: tailored, not off-the-shelf.
- We combine latest technology expertise with local context.
- Premium, trustworthy, forward-looking brand voice.

**Tone:** Confident, clear, ambitious but grounded. Avoid jargon; lead with outcomes and partnership.

## 3. Global changes

- **Layout metadata:** Title/description reflect “visionary startup” and “every enterprise and industry can harness AI”.
- **Navbar:** Tagline optional; “Get Started” CTA kept; ensure active states use brand (e.g. brand-teal).
- **Footer:** One-line vision (“Ensuring every enterprise and industry in Bangladesh can harness AI”), same links; newsletter copy aligned.
- **Design system:** Keep existing (Plus Jakarta, Source Sans 3, Syne; section-label, section-muted, gradient-mesh-bg, brand colors). Add any premium touches (e.g. subtle motion, larger typography hierarchy, more whitespace) per section.

### 4.2 About (`/about`)

- **Hero:** “About SocioFi” — We’re on a mission to ensure every enterprise and industry in Bangladesh can harness AI.
- **Our story:** Timeline/milestones; imagery: about-story, team, product/industry.
- **Mission & vision:** Clear statements: e.g. Vision = “A Bangladesh where every enterprise and industry harnesses AI”; Mission = “Build the right AI solution for every client using the latest technology and local context.”
- **Our approach:** How we work — discovery, design, build, iterate; section-label + cards.
- **Our values:** Innovation, integrity, impact, inclusion (or similar); premium cards.
- **Our commitment:** To clients, to Bangladesh, to next-gen builders; imagery.
- **Team:** “Meet the team”; cards with photos (about-team or generated).
- **CTA:** Same gradient CTA as home.

### 4.3 Services (`/services` + subpages)

- **Main page:** Hero “Our services” — From custom agents to full-stack AI strategy; we build the right solution for your industry. Process steps + 4 service cards linking to subpages.
- **Subpages (Agent development, Industry solutions, Consultancy, Incubation):** Each with hero (section-label + headline + short value prop), “What we offer”, “Why this”, imagery (service-agents, industry-garment, service-consultancy, service-incubation), CTA.

### 4.4 Products (`/products` + subpages)

- **Main page:** Hero “Our products”; product grid with real images (product-fabricxai, internal-app, chatbot, newswise, aingyan, etc.). Each card: image, name, one-line description, “Learn more”.
- **Subpages:** Hero + features/benefits + demo/use cases; imagery per product; CTA “Get in touch” or “See services”.

### 4.5 Careers (`/careers`)

- **Hero:** “Careers at SocioFi” — Build the future of AI with a visionary team in Bangladesh.
- **Why work here:** Benefits (mission-driven, innovation, growth, impact, culture, flexibility); image careers-office.
- **Life at SocioFi:** Testimonials + “Team activities” image grid (careers-office, about-team, etc.).
- **Open positions:** List or “We’re hiring” CTA.
- **CTA:** Join us — link to contact or positions.

### 4.6 Blog (`/blog`)

- **Hero:** “Ideas and updates” — On AI in Bangladesh and what we’re building.
- **Featured + latest:** Real images (blog-ai or generated); section-label “Blog”; card style consistent with site.

### 4.7 Contact (`/contact`)

- **Hero:** “Get in touch” — Let’s build the right solution together.
- **Form + info + map/social:** Premium form styling; contact info; optional map.
